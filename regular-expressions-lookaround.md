### Positive lookahead
`q(?=u)` matches a "q" that is followed by a "u"

### Negative lookahead
`q(?!u)`  matches a "q" not followed by a "u"

### Positive lookbehind
`(?<=a)b` matches a "b" that is preceded by an "a"

### Negative lookbehind
`(?<!a)b` matches a "b" that is not preceded by an "a"
